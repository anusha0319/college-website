        <h2>Introduction</h2>
        <p>
            In fulfilling its academic mission, the institution pursues three core objectives: <strong>Effective Teaching</strong>, <strong>Meaningful Research</strong>, and <strong>Community Service</strong>. These pillars uphold the preservation and expansion of knowledge through inquiry and innovation. The program, program-specific, and course outcomes provide clear direction for achieving these goals.
        </p>
        <p>
            At the start of every academic year, the Academic Committee engages with department heads to review Program Outcomes (POs), Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs), and Course Outcomes (COs). Faculty are informed and aligned with these objectives during departmental planning sessions.
        </p>

        <h2>Program Outcomes (POs)</h2>
        <ol>
            <li><strong>Logical and Critical Thinking:</strong> Gain fundamental and advanced knowledge to become competent and creative professionals.</li>
            <li><strong>Effective Interpersonal Skills:</strong> Improve communication and build strong professional relationships.</li>
            <li><strong>Management Skills:</strong> Apply management principles to solve operational and technical problems using modern tools and simulations.</li>
            <li><strong>Technological Orientation:</strong> Stay current with emerging trends and technologies in computing and related fields.</li>
            <li><strong>Ethics and Social Responsibility:</strong> Uphold professional ethics and contribute to society through responsible behavior and teamwork.</li>
            <li><strong>Lifelong Learning:</strong> Embrace continuous learning and stay informed about contemporary issues in the discipline.</li>
            <li><strong>Communication Skills:</strong> Effectively convey ideas in both written and oral forms.</li>
            <li><strong>Time Management:</strong> Set goals, plan effectively, meet deadlines, and deliver quality results under pressure.</li>
            <li><strong>Cultural Competence:</strong> Collaborate across diverse teams and understand global and cultural perspectives in professional settings.</li>
            <li><strong>Career Planning:</strong> Understand and articulate personal career goals and pathways for development.</li>
        </ol>
